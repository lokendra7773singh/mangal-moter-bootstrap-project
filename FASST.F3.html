The Power To Keep Going Strong

The high-capacity Lithium-Ion LFP battery boasts waterproof and dustproof feature. Its powerful motor, combined with its two-way radio series, provides outstanding temperature characteristics, even in extremely hot and cold environments. This unique chemistry of the LFP battery provides a stable output for extended operating time, regardless of the conditions.

With You For A Long Time

Faast F3 offers a 2500W motor combined with 3.53kWh dual batteries that feature switchable technology to extend battery life. With an impressive 125 km range, this scooter is perfect for long rides.

Go Beyond!

This one-of-a-kind e-scooter offers a top speed of 70 km/hr which is ideal for city & long rides. Not only this, but it also has 12 inch tubeless tyres, telescopic suspension and Spring Loaded hydraulic Shock Absorber.

More Intelligent. More Secure

With an amazing Wheel Lock Feature, eliminates the anxiety of scooter theft. If someone tries to push this scooter when it is locked, the wheels get automatically locked.

Be Safe

The Faast F3 is equipped with 110-130mm combi brakes, which provides an extra layer of safety for the rider. These brakes automatically regulate the use of the front brakes when the rear brakes are applied, eliminating the need for the rider to worry about using the front brakes separately.
